Green Screen Studio Hire

Voyager Studios is a pioneering venture designed to cater to the demand for high-quality video production services, utilising professional green screen technology and equipment. Our mission is to provide podcasters, content creators, and businesses with a versatile and cost-effective solution for creating immersive video content. By leveraging cutting-edge technology and expert freelance services, Voyager Studios aims to become the go-to facility for all top quality green screen production needs.

What We Do

We offer a comprehensive range of services, including green screen rental, professional lighting, audio equipment, and pre- and post-production facilities. Our studio caters to a diverse clientele, from independent podcasters and YouTubers to SME’s and advertising agencies. The studio's strategic location in a vibrant city centre ensures easy access from the train station and visibility.

Our Virtual production combines real-time tech, digital environments, and virtual assets, offering podcasters and vloggers many benefits. It enhances visual appeal by enabling high-quality, diverse content without physical sets, allowing greater creative freedom. It's cost effective, reducing travel and expensive shoots, making professional production more accessible. We can shoot in any environment, offering endless storytelling possibilities. Virtual production also speeds up timelines with real-time rendering and editing, allowing quicker turnarounds and dynamic visuals to improve audience engagement.

How It Works (4 Steps)

  • step 01

    Check Availability

    Begin by checking the availability of our greenscreen studio for your preferred date and time. Our online booking system makes it easy to see what slots are open, allowing you to plan your production with confidence.
  • step 02

    Confirm Equipment

    Once your booking is confirmed, you can select the equipment you wish to hire. We offer a range of professional-grade cameras, lighting setups, and sound gear tailored to meet the needs of your project. Simply choose the items that best fit your production requirements.
  • step 03

    Choose Support Services

    To enhance your production, you can choose from a variety of support services provided by our network of collaborators through Creative Collaborators. Whether you need assistance with camera operation, lighting, sound engineering, or creative direction, we connect you with experienced professionals who can help bring your vision to life.
  • step 04

    Production Day

    On the day of your shoot, our team will have everything prepared according to your selections. You'll have access to the greenscreen studio, the equipment you've chosen, and the support services you’ve requested, ensuring a smooth and efficient production process.
